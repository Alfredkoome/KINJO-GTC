Prayer Ministry

The Prayer Ministry is the spiritual backbone of our church, dedicated to seeking God's presence through prayer, intercession, and worship. We believe that prayer is essential to the life of every believer and the growth of the church. Through prayer, we bring our needs before God, stand in the gap for others, and seek His guidance in all that we do.

The prayer intercessors meet every Thursday evening at the church for a special time of prayer, worship, and intercession. During these gatherings, members pray for the church, families, leaders, the community, and various needs presented by believers. These meetings provide an opportunity to grow spiritually and strengthen one another in faith.

In addition to the weekly intercessory meetings, daily prayer services are held at the church every morning at 5:00 AM. These early morning prayers bring believers together to seek God's direction, give thanks, and commit each day into His hands. Through consistent prayer, we experience God's power, peace, and faithfulness in our lives.

Our vision is to cultivate a church that is rooted in prayer, dependent on God, and passionate about interceding for others. We desire to see lives transformed, faith strengthened, and God's will accomplished through the power of prayer. We welcome all who desire to grow in their prayer life and join us as we seek the Lord together.
