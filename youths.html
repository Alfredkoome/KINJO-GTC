Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry is a dynamic and exciting part of our church, dedicated to helping young people grow in their faith, develop strong Christian character, and build meaningful friendships. Our desire is to see every young person develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ and become a positive influence in the church, their families, and the community.

The youth meet every Sunday after the church service for fellowship, encouragement, and various activities. These gatherings provide an opportunity for young people to learn from God's Word, share experiences, and strengthen their faith together. The ministry also holds practice sessions that help develop teamwork, discipline, and active participation in church programs.

In addition to spiritual growth activities, the youth occasionally organize special days for cleaning and maintaining the church environment. Through these acts of service, they learn responsibility, teamwork, and the importance of caring for God's house.

The Youth Ministry also participates in church celebrations and special events throughout the year. During the December celebrations, the youth come together to prepare and enjoy chapati, creating memorable moments of fellowship, joy, and unity.

Our vision is to raise a generation of young people who love God, live according to His Word, and serve others with passion and commitment. We seek to encourage youth to discover their gifts, develop their talents, and use them for the glory of God while making a positive impact in the world around them.
