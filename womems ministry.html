Women's Ministry

The Women's Ministry is a vibrant and important part of our church family, dedicated to helping women grow spiritually, build meaningful relationships, and serve God with joy and commitment. We believe that every woman has a unique purpose in God's plan, and our desire is to create an environment where women can be encouraged, strengthened, and equipped to live according to His Word.

The ministry provides opportunities for prayer, Bible study, fellowship, and mutual support. Through these activities, women are able to deepen their faith, share life's experiences, and encourage one another in their walk with Christ. We meet every Thursday at 3:00 PM for a time of fellowship, prayer, and spiritual encouragement, where women gather to learn from God's Word and grow together as sisters in Christ.

In addition to our regular meetings, the Women's Ministry participates in various church activities and special events throughout the year. The women occasionally come together to prepare meals and serve others, demonstrating love, hospitality, and the spirit of Christian service. These moments help strengthen unity within the ministry while providing practical ways to minister to the church and the wider community.

Our vision is to raise women who are rooted in faith, committed to prayer, and passionate about serving God. We seek to encourage women to use their gifts and talents for the glory of God, to be positive influences in their homes and communities, and to support one another through every season of life. Through God's grace, we strive to build a ministry where women can grow spiritually, develop lasting friendships, and make a meaningful impact for the Kingdom of God.
